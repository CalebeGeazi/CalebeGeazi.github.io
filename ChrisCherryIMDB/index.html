Christopher Emerson Cherry, (born May 17, 1990) is an American actor, musician, and songwriter. He made his acting debut in the Apple and Eve "100% Juice 100% Imagination" commercial on March 4th, 2021. He was able to enjoy his debut with his twin daughters Ariya and Dillyn. His manager, who is also his highschool sweetheart and wife (Lexi Cherry), has made it known to Hollywood that the Cherrys are ready to take on the film industry by storm. When he's not acting, Chris enjoys time with his wife and 3 daughters, the ultimate Girl Dad. He is also the Creative Director at Imapct Church in Medford, MA... See full bio »
